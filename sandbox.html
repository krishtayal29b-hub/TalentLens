Reproducible ranking sandbox

Rank real candidates against the Senior AI Engineer JD — live, in this browser.

This runs the same feature-based scoring pipeline as our offline rank.py (ported line-for-line to JS), against the real Redrob candidate schema. No hosted LLM calls, no GPU, nothing sent over the network — everything below happens locally in your browser in well under a second.

100,000 candidates scored offline — honeypots flagged

1. Choose a candidate sample

Use the bundled 50-candidate sample from the released dataset, or upload your own JSON file (an array of candidate objects matching candidate_schema.json, max 100 candidates — this mirrors the hackathon's sandbox requirement).

2. Run the ranker

Scores each candidate against the JD's hard requirements (embeddings, vector search, ranking/eval frameworks, production Python), penalizes keyword-stuffed non-engineering titles, consulting-only careers, and LangChain-only "trap" profiles, and blends in Redrob behavioral signals (response rate, recency, notice period). Honeypot profiles are detected and forced out of the ranking.
